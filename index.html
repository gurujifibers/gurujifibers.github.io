    About Us

    We are based in Bhopal (M.P.), India. We manufacture fibreglass products to suit various applications in large quantities. Our products include FRP Manhole Cover Mould, FRP Garden Bench Mould, FRP Bio-Toilet, FRP Planter Mould, FRP Concrete Mould, FRP POP Designing Mould, FRP Baluster Mould, FRP Bollard Mould, FRP Tree Guard Mould and FRP Customized Moulds. A sound infrastructure with trained manpower ensures manufacture of FRP products in large quantities. Moreover, we follow moral business policies and crystal pure transparency in all our transactions to keep healthy relations with the customers.
